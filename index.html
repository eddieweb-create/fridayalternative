What we're about

The Friday Alternative is Warrington's only proper indie and alternative club night. Not a room in the corner of a pub. Not a playlist on shuffle. A real night, with people who care about music.

We play everything from 80s post-punk and Britpop to modern alt, post-punk revival, electronic, and hip-hop — as long as it's good, it's on.

Resident DJs plus hand-picked guests. Dark room. Loud soundsystem. The kind of crowd that sings every word and argues about B-sides.
